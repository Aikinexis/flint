What Was Fixed

The issue was that the panels were saving history items to IndexedDB storage, but not updating the app state. This meant:

  • ‚úÖ History was being saved to storage
  • ‚ùå History component wasn't receiving updates in real-time
  • ‚ùå You had to reload the panel to see new history items

Solution: Updated all three panels (Generate, Rewrite, Summary) to call actions.addHistoryItem() after saving to storage, which updates the app state and triggers a re-render of the History component.

Files Modified

  • src/components/GeneratePanel.tsx
  • src/components/RewritePanel.tsx
  • src/components/SummaryPanel.tsx

Expected Behavior

After performing any AI operation:

  1. The operation is saved to IndexedDB storage
  2. The app state is updated with the new history item
  3. The History component receives the updated state
  4. The new item appears immediately in the history list
  5. No page reload required
